About Me.

With over 10 years of IT experience, I have developed a strong foundation in designing and building responsive, feature-rich web applications using a wide array of front-end and back-end technologies. My expertise includes Object-Oriented JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS, VueJs, AngularJS, and the MERN stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, and NodeJS). I excel in all stages of the software development lifecycle, from requirement analysis and planning to design, implementation, and testing. My experience in developing web and mobile applications has been complemented by a solid understanding of content management systems like WordPress and dotCMS, along with dynamic state management using ReactJS and GraphQL.

I am adept at utilizing cloud technologies, particularly AWS, where I have managed deployments on services like EC2, S3, and RDS. My technical skill set also includes developing RESTful web services and microservices using languages such as Golang, as well as hands-on experience with DevOps practices and CI/CD tools like Jenkins. Furthermore, I follow Test-Driven Development (TDD) methodologies, employing tools like Jest and Jasmine to ensure high-quality code. With a strong commitment to delivering robust and scalable solutions, I thrive in collaborative environments, leveraging agile methodologies to adapt to changing priorities and project requirements.
